# Introduction letter — SDLE Study Path

**To every dental graduate preparing for the KSA SDLE (SCFHS / Prometric),**

This app is a free, open-source **study path** — not official exam software. It was built so you can study on a laptop or phone browser without jumping between random PDFs, Telegram banks, and half-finished notes.

## What you get

1. **A clear plan** — 14 deep content lessons, stretched across 14 / 30 / 45 / 60 / 90 calendar days if you need more time.  
2. **Reading inside the app** — long-form lessons (ADHD-friendly blocks). You do not need the raw textbook PDFs open for the daily path.  
3. **MCQs & quizzes** — thousands of practice items, volume packs (50 / 100 / 150 / ALL), wrong book, timed mocks, and an MCQs hub.  
4. **Always-comes free points** — high-yield rules that keep showing up.  
5. **Videos on Google Drive** — links only (no huge video files to download with the site).  
6. **Progress on your device** — scores and wrong book stay in your browser (same idea as studying on one computer).

## How to start (2 minutes)

1. Open the live site: **https://xxxova2.github.io/sdle-study-path/**  
2. Choose a **plan length** on **Today**.  
3. Do **Step 1 Read** → **Step 2 Videos (Drive)** → cards / quiz.  
4. Aim for practice accuracy **≥80%**. Official pass is about **542/800** — this app is your coach, not the real exam.

## Where is Endodontics?

**Endo is its own subject — not under Restorative.**

| Days | Subject |
|------|---------|
| 1–4 | Restorative (operative, fixed, RPD/CD, resto mega) |
| **5** | Periodontics |
| **6** | **Endodontics + trauma** |
| 7–8 | OMS / path / ethics |
| 9 | Ortho + pedo |

In **Extra practice** or **MCQs**, tap the **Endo** buttons (separate pool).
