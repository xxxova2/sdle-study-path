# Notices

## What this project is

A **static browser study app** for the KSA SDLE (Saudi Dental Licensure Examination) path:
in-app readings, MCQ practice, flashcards, and plan tabs. Videos open on **Google Drive**
(links only — no video files in this repository).

## What is not in this repository

- Textbook **PDF files** and large source dumps (`data/raw/` is gitignored)
- Local lecture **video files** (use Drive links in the app)

## Study content

Lesson text and practice questions in `data/*.js` are study materials assembled for personal
exam prep. They may reference publicly known clinical facts and community-shared exam-style
items. **This is not official SCFHS / Prometric software.** Do not treat it as an authorized
redistribution of any commercial course, bank, or publisher PDF.

If you are a rights holder and believe content should be removed, open a GitHub issue.
